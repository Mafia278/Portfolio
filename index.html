Snake Game

I have meticulously crafted a coal-themed iteration of the timeless Snake game, seamlessly blending nostalgia with innovation. Notepad

I am pleased to present my project on a notepad application integrated with Huffman coding, showcasing a fusion of data structures and algorithms in a practical software implementation. This project encapsulates the essence of efficient data compression techniques while offering a user-friendly text editing interface. Through meticulous design and implementation, the notepad demonstrates proficiency in utilizing complex algorithms to enhance everyday software functionalities.
